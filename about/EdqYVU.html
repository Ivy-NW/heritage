Our Mission

Heritage is passionate about language revitalization and cultural experiences. We use cutting-edge AI technology to help indigenous communities revive endangered languages and traditions.

Our Vision

Heritage envisions a vibrant, interconnected world where all cultures and languages thrive. We are committed to empowering communities to preserve their heritage for future generations through immersive language and cultural experiences.

Our Team

Our team comprises linguists, anthropologists, engineers and community leaders passionate about cultural preservation. We collaborate closely with indigenous communities to co-create personalized solutions that meet their unique needs and goals.
