What Can Cleo Do?
Our intelligent chatbot Cleo can answer customer questions, provide product recommendations, and guide users through account setup and other processes. She uses natural language processing to understand questions and provide helpful responses.
What Languages Does Cleo Speak?
Cleo speaks fluent English and Spanish. We plan to expand her language capabilities over time to assist more global customers.
Is My Chat History Saved?
For your privacy and security, Cleo does not store chat history or personal data. Conversations are ephemeral and disappear after each session ends.
Who Can Use Cleo?
Cleo is designed to help anyone who visits our site or interfaces with our customer service channels. There are no age restrictions, though parental guidance is suggested for minors.
How Do I Contact a Human Agent?
If Cleo is unable to fully address your needs, simply say "Speak to an agent" during your conversation and you will be connected to a human team member right away.
Will Cleo Replace Human Agents?
No, Cleo works alongside our supportive customer care team to provide quick answers and seamless hand-offs when needed. Her role is to enhance, not replace, human connections.
